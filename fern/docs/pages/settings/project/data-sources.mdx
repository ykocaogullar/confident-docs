Data sources let you connect external platforms to Confident AI so you can automatically generate evaluation datasets from your existing documents and messages, rather than creating goldens manually. Once connected, Confident AI syncs your content on a schedule and uses it to produce goldens for your datasets.

Currently supported data sources:

- **Google Drive** - Retrieves documents from a specific folder
- **SharePoint** - Accesses documents from Microsoft SharePoint
- **Slack** - Syncs messages from Slack channels
- **Notion** - Fetches content from Notion pages and databases

## Create a Data Source

1. Navigate to **Project Settings** > **Data Sources**
2. Click **New Data Source**
3. Enter a unique **Name** for the data source
4. Select a **Type** from the dropdown (Google Drive, SharePoint, Slack, or Notion)
5. Click **Save**

## Configure Credentials

After creating a data source, you need to provide credentials so Confident AI can access the external platform. Click into the data source to open its detail page and fill in the required fields.

**Google Drive** requires:
- **Service Account JSON** - Credentials from a Google Cloud service account
- **Folder URL** - URL of the Google Drive folder to sync

**SharePoint** requires:
- **Tenant ID** - Azure tenant identifier
- **Client ID** - Azure app registration client ID
- **Client Secret** - Azure app registration secret

**Slack** requires:
- **Bot Token** - Slack bot token (starts with `xoxb-`)

**Notion** requires:
- **Integration Token** - Notion integration secret token

## Connect and Sync

Once credentials are saved, click **Connect** to establish the connection. After connecting, you have two options for keeping data up to date:

- **Manual sync** - Click **Sync** to pull the latest data at any time
- **Automatic sync** - Configure a sync schedule so data stays fresh without manual intervention

### Sync Schedule

You can set the sync frequency to one of the following intervals:

- Every hour
- Every 2 hours
- Every 6 hours (default)
- Every 12 hours
- Every 24 hours

Toggle **Sync Enabled** on or off to control whether automatic syncing is active.

## Dataset Generation

Here's the thing: data sources on their own just sync content. The real value comes when you link a data source to a dataset's generation configuration. Confident AI will then process the synced content and automatically produce test cases (goldens) for your evaluation datasets.

## Manage Data Sources

From the data sources list, use the action buttons on any row to **Sync**, **Edit**, or **Delete** a data source.

To disconnect a data source without deleting it, open the detail page and click **Disconnect**. This removes the connection but preserves your configuration.
